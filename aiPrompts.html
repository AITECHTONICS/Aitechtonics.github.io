Image Generation Prompt

Prompt Template:

Create an image of [Title/Subject]. The scene should be set in [Context and Setting], featuring [Key Elements and Details]. Use a [Color Scheme and Lighting] to convey the desired mood. Capture the scene from [Perspective and Composition], ensuring the focus is on [specific element or area]. The image should have a [Style and Artistic Direction] to enhance its visual appeal. Include subjects that are [Subject Expressions and Actions] to add dynamism to the scene. [Additional Notes].

Prompt Example:

Create an image of a serene mountain village at sunrise. The scene should be set in a quaint village nestled in a valley surrounded by towering snow-capped mountains. The village features rustic wooden cottages with smoke gently rising from their chimneys, cobblestone paths, and a small flowing river that reflects the early morning light. Use a color palette of soft pastels, including warm pinks, oranges, and cool blues, to depict the gentle morning light and the tranquil atmosphere. The lighting should create a soft glow over the mountains and illuminate the rooftops of the cottages, casting long shadows on the ground. Capture the scene from a low-angle perspective from the edge of the village, looking up towards the mountains with the village in the foreground and the sun just beginning to rise behind the peaks. The image should have a realistic, painterly style, emphasizing the natural beauty and peacefulness of the mountain setting. Include subjects that are villagers starting their day, some fetching water from the river, while others are tending to their gardens, adding a sense of daily life and community. Ensure the image has a high resolution to capture the intricate details of the mountain landscape and the texture of the village buildings.
